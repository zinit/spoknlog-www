Frequently asked

Does SpoknLog join the meeting and record it?

+

No. SpoknLog is built for the recap right after the call. You speak the parts that matter — decisions, owners, deadlines — and SpoknLog turns that into a searchable note. There is no bot, no calendar integration, and no live recording.

Can I ask about a meeting days or weeks later?

+

Yes. Every recap becomes part of your searchable memory. Ask "what did we agree about the budget?" or "who was supposed to send the contract?" and the answer is generated from your own notes.

Is the audio of my recap stored?

+

No. The audio is transcribed on Microsoft Azure in your region and then permanently deleted. Only the transcription, AI summary, and search index are kept.

Does it work in my language?

+

SpoknLog supports English, Polish, German, Spanish, French, Italian, Slovak, Romanian, and Ukrainian. Recaps are transcribed in the language you speak, and answers come back in the same language.
