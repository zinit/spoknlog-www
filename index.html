How it works

Three steps. Zero learning curve. Works from day one.

Step 01

Say it

Finish a client call. Walk to the car. Tap and say: "Agreed on 15k, send contract by Friday." A grocery list. A midnight idea. Just speak — done.

Step 02

Forget about it

SpoknLog figures out if you're saving a thought or asking a question. No folders, no tags, no decisions. Just talk.

Step 03

Ask for it back

"What was I supposed to buy?" — ask in your own words. SpoknLog finds the answer across everything you've ever recorded.

How your data actually flows

We use AI to give you superpowers. Here's exactly what happens with your data — no hand-waving.

You speak

Transcribed, then deleted

Your voice is transcribed on Microsoft Azure in your region (EU or US). The audio is then permanently deleted. We never store, archive, or copy recordings.

AI works for you

Classified, summarized, indexed

Your transcription is analyzed by AI on Azure enterprise infrastructure — classified, summarized, and made searchable. No human ever reads your content. No provider trains models on your data.

You ask

Answers from your notes only

When you ask a question, AI searches your notes to find the answer — from your data only. The response is generated and spoken back in real time.

What we keep

Transcription text, AI summary, search index

What we delete

Audio — immediately after transcription

What we never do

Sell data, train models, show to humans, require identity

SpoknLog uses AI to remember what you said and remind you when it matters. Audio deleted. No account required. No data sold. The AI works for you — nobody else.
