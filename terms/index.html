Section 10. Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI)

  1. The Provider informs that the Application uses artificial intelligence technologies to provide the Service:
    1. speech-to-text transcription — Whisper models, provided by Microsoft Corporation through the Azure OpenAI Service,

    3. intent classification, summary generation, and response generation — GPT models (OpenAI, L.L.C.; Microsoft Corporation), Gemini (Google LLC), Qwen and GPT-OSS (Groq, Inc.),
    4. text-to-speech synthesis — Google Cloud Text-to-Speech (Google LLC) and Azure Text-to-Speech (Microsoft Corporation) services,

  2. The User acknowledges that results generated by AI:
    1. may contain errors, inaccuracies, or AI hallucinations (generation of content not grounded in fact),
    2. require human verification before use,
    3. do not constitute legal, medical, financial, or any other professional advice.
  3. The User undertakes to:
    1. verify all AI-generated results before using them,
    2. not rely exclusively on generated content when making decisions,
    3. comply with the AI Act transparency requirements with respect to third parties to whom they share AI-generated content.
  4. Data entered into the Application is processed in accordance with the two-tier model described in the Privacy Policy:
    1. voice recordings (audio) are transmitted to the Provider’s server and then forwarded to the Azure OpenAI Service solely for the purpose of obtaining a transcription; after the transcription is obtained, the recording is permanently deleted; the Provider does not store audio recordings,
    2. transcriptions and semantic vectors (Tier 1) are processed exclusively on Microsoft Azure infrastructure in the region corresponding to the User’s location (European Union or United States),
    3. derived data, such as summaries and search contexts (Tier 2), may be forwarded to external AI providers listed in subsection 1, item 3 for the purpose of generating responses,
    4. text-to-speech synthesis (TTS) is performed on text generated by the Provider’s system, not on raw User data,
    5. none of the AI providers use User data for training their models.
  5. To the extent permitted by applicable law, the Provider shall not be liable for:
    1. decisions made on the basis of AI-generated results,
    2. damage arising from incorrect or incomplete responses generated by AI.
  6. AI-generated content (in particular note summaries and answers to questions) constitutes part of User Content to the extent permitted by law. The User acknowledges that:
    1. AI-generated content may not be eligible for copyright protection,
    2. similar content may be generated for other users, and the Provider does not guarantee the uniqueness of results.
