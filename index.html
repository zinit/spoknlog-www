What happens after you hit record

Most voice apps send your recordings straight to the cloud. We don't. Here's how your data actually flows.

Your voice

Transcribed and deleted

Your voice is transcribed on secure Microsoft Azure servers in your region (EU or US), then permanently deleted. Classification and summaries are processed through trusted AI partners — but only using derived text, never your original words.

Your data

Encrypted and protected

Everything is encrypted in transit and at rest. Stored on secure servers in Europe and the US. You can delete all your data at any time — no questions asked.

AI answers

Minimal, text-only

When you ask a question, only processed text fragments reach AI providers to formulate a response. Never your voice. Never your full notes.

No ads. No tracking. No training AI on your data. We built SpoknLog for ourselves first — we wouldn't use it if we didn't trust it with our own thoughts.
